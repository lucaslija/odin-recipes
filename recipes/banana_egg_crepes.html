Banana-Egg Crepes

Description

This three-ingredient recipe is easy as can be. Simply combine one over-ripe banana, three tablespoons of the flour of your choice, and two eggs to create delicious, healthy banana "pancakes".

Ingredients

  • One (1) banana, very ripe
  • Two (2) eggs
  • Three (3) tablespoons almond flour
  • (Optional) Olive or other oil for frying
  • (Optional) Nut butter & honey for topping
