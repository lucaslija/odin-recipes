Harvest Bowl

Description

This copy of a popular Sweetgreen salad bar recipe tastes like Autumn in a bowl.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups kale
  • 1/2 cup wild rice
  • 1/2 cup chicken
  • 1/4 cup sweet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup apples
  • a dollop of goat cheese
  • a pinch of chopped almonds
  • Balsamic vinaigrette to taste

Steps

  1. Wash, de-vein, and chop the kale into thin strips.
  2. Cook the wild rice in a small rice cooker or saucepan.
  3. Cube the chicken and sweet potatoes and roast both in the oven until browned.
  4. Slice the apple thinly.
  5. Mix kale, rice, chicken, sweet potato, and apple in a large bowl, adding vinaigrette to taste.
  6. Top with goat cheese and almond slices, and enjoy!
