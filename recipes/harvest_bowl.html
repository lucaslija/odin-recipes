Harvest Bowl

Description

This copy of a popular Sweetgreen salad bar recipe tastes like Autumn in a bowl.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1/2 cup wild rice
  • 1/2 cup cubed chicken
  • 1/4 cut sweet potatoes roasted and cubed
  • 1/4 cup sliced apples
  • a dollop of goat cheese
  • a pinch of chopped almonds

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and place it in a bowl.
  2. Mash the banana with a fork until it is a paste.
  3. Crack two eggs into the banana mush one at a time, stirring each in thoroughly.
  4. Add three tablespoons of almond flour, pouring one tablespoon in at a time and stirring thoroughly before moving on to the next.
  5. Scoop up a half cup of the "dough" and place it on a pan heated to medium.
  6. Wait a few minutes, until outside is browned and crispy, before flipping over the pancake.
  7. Repeat on the other side, then serve hot with topping of choice.
